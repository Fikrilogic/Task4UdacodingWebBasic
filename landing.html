ABOUT US

Petaniku is a digital website that was born in the midst of many people.
This website focuses on people's Food needs

With increasing agricultural technology, it causes changes in people's lifestyle. especially in the field of food which is the most important thing for everyone's life. So we came up with an idea of how to create a website for farmers to connect them with customers. So this website was born so that customers can get food directly from farmers at a fairly low price

Best Farmer Website on 2021

Since the virus pandemic hit the whole world, everyone has started working at home and the local government has prevented large numbers of people from congregating in one place. the market is one place where many people are easily infected with the virus
With this website, they don't need to go to the market to buy the food they need. Only need to order through this website, so they can order food directly from farmers without intermediaries at low and healthy prices
